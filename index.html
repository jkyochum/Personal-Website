My story

I have been programming since 2016, when I was introduced to object-oriented programming with C++. I made my way through several different development environments and platforms and cherished the value that I received from every new language I learned. I published my first app in college, and that milestone really fueled my desire to create excellent content for people to enjoy. Fast-forward to 2023. I'm deep in the world of front-end web development. My goal is to absorb as much as I possibly can because it's fascinating and it empowers me to express my creativity however I see it. I now believe this is my true calling: to give people the beauty and simplicity they desire while seamlessly integrating the technicalities underneath. It has been a fun ride so far, and I look forward to whatever happens next.
